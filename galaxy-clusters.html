Galaxy Cluster Simulations

Having a closer look at the Local Universe galaxy clusters

The Coma Cluster

Coma is a massive, dynamically highly active galaxy cluster in our Local Universe. Its unique internal structure, with two main galaxies at the center, makes it a highly interesting cluster. Zoom-in simulations enable a detailed study of the low redshift properties of this cluster as well as its formation path and assembly process.
