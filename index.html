Hello and Welcome!

I am an AI Researcher at the appliedAI Institute for Europe (TransferLab), where I work on advancing machine learning research and its applications across scientific domains.

I completed my Ph.D. at Ludwig Maximilian University in Munich within the Computational Astrophysics Research Group, focusing on developing and analyzing cosmological simulations to study structure formation in the universe.

In spring 2024 I was a guest researcher at the Flatiron Institute of the Simons Foundation, integrating machine learning with cosmological simulations and galaxy cluster data.
